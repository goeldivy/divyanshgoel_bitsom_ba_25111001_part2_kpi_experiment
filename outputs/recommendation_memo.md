# Recommendation Memo

## Executive Summary

The experiment compared the existing onboarding experience (Control) with a new onboarding experience (Treatment) to determine whether the new experience improves business performance.

## North Star Metric

Paid Conversion Rate

## Experiment Summary

The Treatment group demonstrated improved onboarding completion, paid conversion, engagement score, and average revenue per user compared with the Control group.

## Guardrail Metrics

The following guardrail metrics were evaluated:

* Refund Rate
* Support Ticket Rate
* Average Days to Convert

These metrics should continue to be monitored after deployment to ensure that conversion improvements do not negatively impact customer experience.

## Recommendation

Launch the new onboarding experience while continuing to monitor guardrail metrics during rollout.

## Risks

* Increased support requests after launch.
* Possible differences across customer segments.
* Long-term retention was not measured in this experiment.

## Next Steps

* Continue monitoring key business metrics.
* Perform segment-level analysis after rollout.
* Validate long-term customer retention and revenue impact.
